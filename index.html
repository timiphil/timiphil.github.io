Information About me

I'm a fitness trainer and former SEO specialist who enjoys creating things with code.

I became fascinated with programming for the first time in 2020 during pandemic. I was in Tallinn with my programmer friend when he told me about Javascript and how it could automate things. I was hooked as a person who seeks efficiency.

I started with Javascript, but it was too complicated for me, so I simply focus on SEO. Unfortunately, that didn't go very far since my heart is constantly captivated by how coders build things on the internet. I attempted it again in 2021, but this time I had very little time to study and let programming slide.

However, when the present European war conflict began, I was in Ukraine with my wife, and we managed to survive and move to another European nation. Then I realised it was time to do what I love, no matter what it takes. So I resumed my studies. I specifically learnt web development because I wanted to build things for myself.

After a few months of self-study, I decided this was the path I wanted to take with my career. So I enrolled in the Freecodecamp Bootcamp and poured my heart and soul into it. Every day, late into the night, and on weekends, I was coding. Until I was ready to work on production code.

Since the boot camp, I've evolved as a developer by working alongside experienced devs and goal-getters who have helped me elevate my expectations for what a web application should be.

I'm currently looking for a full-time role where I can use my coding and business skills to assist a company reach their objectives.
